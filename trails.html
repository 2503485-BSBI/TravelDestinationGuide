Pine City Resorts & Waterfalls

Pine City is one of the most beautiful places on the outskirts of Islamabad city, famous for its amazing sceneries, sky-high mountains, and cozy weather. Pine City offers 1 and 2 kanal plots, and 15 & 20 kanal farmhouses, an excellent opportunity for every investor.

Pakistan Monument Museum

Pakistan Monument Museum is located in Shakarparian hills in Zero Point Interchange, Islamabad. A wonderful museum that provides insight into Pakistan's history and leadership.

Panjpeer Rocks

Nestled at an elevation of 5500 feet in the serene landscapes of Union Council Narar, Panjpeer Rocks is a must-visit destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Lotus Lake

Inaugurated in late 2020 as part of a “natural family park” project, the site features a 100-foot-long lotus pond, surrounded by wooden seating areas and a place designated for barbecue. The surroundings has been covered with lattices for security purpose, also aiming to preserve the natural beauty of the area.
